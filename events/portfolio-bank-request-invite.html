Evening Panel

(Invite-only)

Digital Asset Portfolio Growth, Banking and Custody

While opponents of digital assets site Bitcoin's drop of 60% from its peak, proponents quote its near 60% growth yoy and top hedge funds that have produced double, triple, even quadruple digit returns.

Join BCI's panelists from some of the top performing digital asset funds in the world as they discuss optimal digital asset investment strategies, hedging and diversification techniques. Our second panel will entail a deep dive into digital asset banking and custody issues, solutions and opportunities.

Who we are
BCI was established to provide trust, transparency and targeted networking in the disruptive technology space.
We ensure that long-term relationships are cultivated and maintained within our global community.

Topics
1) Producing positive returns in bull, bear and sideways markets

2) Hedging volatility, liquidity and regulatory changes

3) OTC, Futures or Exchanges - best fills and performance

4) Banking opportunities and drawbacks with digital assets across the globe

5) Custody vs physical holding costs, risks and benefits

6) Regulatory outlook on digital assets - US and around the world
