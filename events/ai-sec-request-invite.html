Rising Threats and Opportunities in AI, Cyber Security and Quantum Computing

While AI/ML has been around for many years, recent developments have been groundbreaking in sectors ranging from finance to military and healthcare. Nevertheless, renowned sector experts such as Stephen Hawking and Elon Musk have long touted the dangers of AI and damage it could cause to society. While we believe these dangers are not imminent, there are many issues that already impact our daily lives.

Join BCI's AI experts as they discuss the current threats and opportunities that are emerging with improvements in technologies and their use cases.
