Threat to Proactive Control - with Endpoint Security

4pm ET, April 29th, 2021 | Virtual

With the dramatic increase in distributed workforces and the growing adoption of cloud applications, companies face unprecedented levels of IP, data, and identity sprawl beyond the enterprise firewall. Every endpoint is an entry into your business, cybercriminals have more ways to break in than ever before, and human error on the inside is a constant risk. Historically, endpoint security has been a zero-sum game—with the odds inevitably stacked against IT. Learn how leading security experts are protecting and improving security controls on local and remote devices to mitigate countless risks.
