About BCI

BCI Summit is a private networking organization for the world’s leading tech executives.

BCI helps participants gain insights from their peers, network with world’s leading executives, develop partnerships, and learn about critical technologies through in-person gatherings, virtual roundtables, and ongoing executive collaboration programs.

All BCI programs and engagements are closed-door, invite-only with no media, nor recordings.
