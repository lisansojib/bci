EDC Membership

2020-2021 Cohort

BCI’s Membership program is designed to gather insights to most pressing challenges, stay informed on newest tech advances and build and long lasting business relationships with technology peers.

Requirements

All access to the EDC program is invite only.

Members must be in one of following verticals

  • Banking
  • Financial Services
  • Insurance
  • Private Investor/CVC
  • Pharma
  • Healthtech
  • Gov/Aerospace/Defence

A minimum time requirement of 30 mins every 2 weeks to partake in panels/networking.

Agenda Overview

Bimonthly panels and networking sessions using our proprietary software for secure communications with your 200 peers across industries. In person Summits, Seminars and Dinners in NYC, all included.

Program Features

1) Panels

  • Topics chosen by group to address member's most pressing issues
  • Panels comprised of members, leading researchers and subject matter experts
  • Research presentations included

2) Curated Group Workshops (in groups of eight)

  • Highly select group of non-competing peers, with similar technology focus
  • Develop trust and strong working relationships with best-in-breed
  • Ongoing bi-monthly group review and problem solving sessions, held virtually and in-person

3) Networking

  • Networking events and activities with full cohort of industry leading peers throughout the year
  • Virtual interactive networking on BCI's secure proprietary software
  • In-person meetings at BCI Enterprise Summits, dinners and panels (upon clearance)

Member Benefits

  • A closed-off cohort of top tech talent to share insights and challenges of nascent technologies.
  • Rigorous educational seminars akin to an Executive Eductation program with non-competing peers from the aforementioned verticals to discuss all aspects of data.
  • Build long term relationships with your annual group through virtual and in-person gatherings in a private, non-competitive environment.
  • Research and presentation by guest researchers in advanced technologies.
  • Program certificate upon completion.

NO MEDIA, NO RECORDINGS
